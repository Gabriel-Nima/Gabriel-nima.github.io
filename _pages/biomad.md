The Dental Biomaterials Laboratory (BioMaD) is part of the [Centro de Investigación e Innovación Biomédica (CiiB)](https://www.uandes.cl/centros/ciib/) at [Universidad de los Andes, Chile](https://www.uandes.cl). The laboratory integrates research in dental materials science, oral microbiology, and digital dentistry, with a focus on developing and evaluating clinically relevant biomaterial-based solutions.  
BioMaD is located at the CiiB building (Av. Monseñor Álvaro del Portillo 12455, Las Condes, Santiago), at the ground floor (zócalo) of the CiiB.

---

## Research Lines

**🦠 Alternative Antimicrobial Therapies & Photodynamic Therapy**
Development and optimization of antimicrobial photodynamic therapy (aPDT) using blue light and curcumin-based photosensitizers as antibiotic-free alternatives for managing oral biofilm-associated diseases, including *Streptococcus mutans* and *Enterococcus faecalis*.

**🔗 Adhesion to Dental Tissues & Restorative Interfaces**
Investigation of bonding mechanisms between restorative materials and dental tissues (enamel and dentin), including the effect of surface treatments, deproteinizing agents, and aging conditions on long-term adhesive performance.

**💡 Polymerization & Light-Curing Technologies**
Evaluation of polymerization kinetics, shrinkage stress, and the influence of LED curing unit design on the mechanical and optical properties of resin-based dental materials, including bulk-fill composites and resin cements.

**🖨️ CAD/CAM & 3D-Printed Dental Materials**
Assessment of accuracy, mechanical behavior, optical properties, and adhesion protocols for additive (3D-printed) and subtractive (milled) dental restorations, including interim and definitive indirect restorations using ceramic-filled resins.

## Equipment & Facilities

BioMaD is equipped for both physical/mechanical testing and microbiological work:

**Mechanical & Physical Testing**
- Universal testing machine (mechanical testing per ISO, ASTM, JIS, DIN and NBR standards)
- Grinding and polishing machine (manual and semi-automatic)
- Precision microcutter (Accutom T10)
- Analytical balance (high-precision gravimetric measurements)
- Binocular loupe

**3D Printing & Digital Workflow**
- Resin 3D printer
- FDM (filament) 3D printer
- UV washing and curing machine (360° UV treatment)

**Microbiological Work**
- Orbital incubator shaker (controlled cell and microorganism culture)
- Ultrasonic processor (sonicator)
- Ultrasonic bath
- Refrigeration system (Visicooler, 4°C, for microbiological samples)
- Refrigerator (4°C / −20°C zones for reagents and biological samples)

**Other**
- Mobile dental unit (air, water, suction systems)
- Electrosurgery unit

Equipment booking is managed through the [QReserve platform](https://www.qreserve.com). Access requires prior training by the responsible researcher or lab manager.
