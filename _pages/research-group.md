## BioMAD – Dental Biomaterials Laboratory

The Dental Biomaterials Laboratory (BioMAD) is part of the Centro de Investigación e Innovación Biomédica (CIIB) at Universidad de los Andes, Chile. The group focuses on the development and evaluation of advanced dental biomaterials and emerging technologies in restorative dentistry.

## Research Focus

Our research activities include:
- Dental biomaterials and biofunctional materials
- Alternative antimicrobial therapies and photodynamic therapy
- Adhesion to dental tissues and restorative interfaces
- Polymerization and light-curing technologies
- CAD/CAM and 3D-printed dental materials
